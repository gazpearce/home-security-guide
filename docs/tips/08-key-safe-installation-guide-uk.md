# Key Safe Installation Guide UK

Complete guide to choosing and installing a key safe for UK homes, covering TS 007 rated products, wall mounting techniques, combination security, and insurance compliance.

---

## Choosing a Key Safe for UK Use

Key safes provide secure external key storage for cleaner access, pet sitters, and emergency access by carers. UK standards for key safes are evolving with Sold Secure and TS 007 ratings. The most secure models have TS 007 3-star rating or Sold Secure Silver/Gold certification. Key safes are typically mechanical push-button combination locks, though electronic keypad and Bluetooth models are increasingly available. Supra C500 and Yale KeySafe are the most popular UK models. Prices range from 25 for basic Yale models at B&Q to 80 for Supra C500 with anti-tamper features. For insurance compliance, choose a Sold Secure approved model. Master Lock and Burton key safes are also widely available at Screwfix and Toolstation.

---

## Installation and Location Guidelines

Choose a location that is weather protected but accessible. Ideal spots: inside a porch, beside the front door under an overhang, or in a side passage out of street view. Mount at chest height for comfortable operation. Fix the key safe to a solid brick or concrete wall using the rawl plugs and screws provided. For timber frames, use 50mm no.10 screws into the frame. Ensure the key safe is not visible from the street or readily accessible through the letterbox. Record the combination in your home security log but not written down next to the safe. Change the combination annually or when codes are shared with new people. Check the key safe mechanism operates smoothly every 3 months and lubricate with silicone spray if needed.

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Are key safes secure enough for insurance requirements?</dt>
<dd>Sold Secure approved key safes meet the security requirements of most UK insurers. Non-rated models may not be accepted. Check with your insurer before installing.</dd>

<dt>What is the most secure key safe brand?</dt>
<dd>Supra C500 with TS 007 3-star and Sold Secure Silver rating is widely regarded as the most secure key safe for UK domestic use, priced at 60-80 from Amazon UK.</dd>

<dt>How often should I change the key safe combination?</dt>
<dd>Change the combination annually or whenever a key is accessed by someone new. Avoid using obvious combinations like year of birth, house number, or sequential numbers. For more information see our <a href="../tips/07-pir-security-lighting-guide.md">PIR Security Lighting Guide</a>.</dd>
</dl>
