# Security Window Sticker Guide

Guide to using security window stickers and property marking for UK homes, covering SelectaDNA, Neighbourhood Watch stickers, visible deterrent placement, and police-endorsed marking schemes.

---

## Property Marking and Warning Stickers

Property marking is recommended by UK police as a cost-effective deterrent. SelectaDNA is the most widely recognised forensic property marking system in the UK. The kit costs 25-50 and contains a unique synthetic DNA code, UV pen, and warning stickers. The DNA solution is applied to valuable items and the warning stickers placed on doors and windows. Burglars avoid marked property because police can scan marked items with UV light and trace them to specific households using the SelectaDNA database. Over 90% of UK police forces participate in SelectaDNA marking programmes. Immobilise is a free online property register supported by the National Police Chiefs' Council. Register serial numbers of all valuables at immobilise.com for free to increase recovered property return rates.

---

## Sticker Placement and Deterrent Strategy

Stickers alone provide limited protection but form part of a layered approach. Place Neighbourhood Watch stickers on or near the front door at eye level. Place alarm warning stickers on ground-floor windows visible from the approach. Place SelectaDNA stickers on front and back doors and ground-floor windows. Place CCTV warning signs at the main entrance pointing toward the camera coverage area. For maximum deterrent effect, stickers should be weatherproof, clearly legible from 2 metres, and show a recognised brand or scheme. Avoid generic security stickers from eBay which lack credibility. Genuine SelectaDNA, Yale, and Ring stickers are available. Replace faded stickers annually to maintain deterrent appearance.

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Does property marking really deter burglars?</dt>
<dd>Yes. Theft of marked property carries higher risk for burglars because police can identify and return marked items. Warning stickers indicating property marking schemes are a significant deterrent factor.</dd>

<dt>What is SelectaDNA and is it worth it?</dt>
<dd>SelectaDNA is a forensic property marking system using unique synthetic DNA codes detectable by police UV scanners. At 25-50, it is widely recommended by UK police and neighbourhood watch schemes.</dd>

<dt>Should I still use stickers if I have a full security system?</dt>
<dd>Yes. Visible stickers reinforce your security measures and provide an immediate visual deterrent before an intruder tests your locks or alarms. Multiple visible security indicators cumulatively deter. For more information see our <a href="../tips/15-dog-security-deterrent-guide.md">Dog Security Deterrent Guide</a>.</dd>
