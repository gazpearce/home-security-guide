# TV Mounting for Rental Properties

Renting a UK property does not mean you cannot wall-mount your TV. This guide covers no-drill solutions, landlord negotiation, damage-free brackets, and removable mounting options for tenants.

---

## No-Drill TV Mounting Solutions for Tenants

UK tenants have several options for mounting a TV without permanently damaging walls. Tension-fit ceiling mounts use a spring-loaded pole between floor and ceiling with a TV bracket attached - suitable for TVs up to 32 inches. Freestanding TV stands are the simplest option with prices from £50 at John Lewis for 55-inch compatible stands. Heavy-duty easel-style stands work for 43-65 inch TVs at £80-£150. Wall-mounted brackets with damage-free adhesive plates use industrial-strength adhesive pads rated for TVs up to 25kg. For TVs over 32 inch the safest no-drill option is a freestanding stand. If the landlord permits drilling use small-diameter screw holes that can be easily filled. Landlord permission for TV mounting: write a formal letter describing the mount type, fixing method, and commitment to professional installation and reinstatement on departure.

---

## Landlord-Friendly TV Mounting Techniques

If your landlord agrees to wall mounting follow these professional techniques. Use cavity fixings that leave the smallest holes. Spring toggle fixings leave 10-12mm holes easily filled with Polyfilla. For dot-dab walls use Gripit fixings that create neat 10mm holes. Never use rawl plugs in plasterboard. Install a mounting plate with keyhole slots allowing easy TV removal without disassembling brackets. Use cable management trunking adhered with 3M Command strips rather than screw-fixed trunking. When vacating the property fill holes with Polyfilla Ready-Mixed (drying 60 minutes) and sand flush with 120-grit paper. Touch up paint using the original colour matched at B&Q or Dulux. Professional reinstatement costs £50-£100 and covers filling, sanding, and painting the repaired area. Many UK letting agents accept TV mounting if the installation is professional and reinstatement is guaranteed in the tenancy agreement.

## Specifications and Comparison

| Rental Mounting Option | Max TV Size | Damage Level | Cost |
| ---|---|---|--- |
| Freestanding stand | Up to 65 inch | None | £50-£150 |
| Tension-fit ceiling mount | Up to 32 inch | None (pressure only) | £60-£100 |
| Adhesive wall mount | Up to 32 inch (15kg max) | None (if correctly removed) | £30-£60 |
| Drilled mount (landlord approved) | Any | Small holes (filled on departure) | £40-£120 |
