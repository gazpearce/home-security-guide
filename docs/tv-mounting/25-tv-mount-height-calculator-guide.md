# TV Mount Height Calculator Guide

Calculate the perfect TV mounting height for comfortable viewing based on screen size, seating distance, and room type. This UK guide provides height formulas, angle calculations, and room-specific recommendations for optimal TV placement.

---

## TV Height Calculation Formula and Standards

The optimal TV centre height for UK homes is 105-115cm from the floor for standard viewing at seated eye level. This places the centre of the screen at the same height as the viewer's eyes when seated on a standard UK sofa (seat height 45cm, seated eye level 105-115cm). Formula: TV centre height = Seated eye level (105cm) + (TV height / 2) - (bottom bezel). For a 55-inch TV with 72cm height: centre = 105cm. Bottom of TV = 105 - 36 = 69cm. Top of TV = 105+36 = 141cm. For above-fireplace installation: fireplace surround height + 50mm clearance + (TV height / 2). For a 55-inch TV above a 95cm fireplace surround: centre = 95+5+36 = 136cm. Viewing distance affects perceived image quality. THX recommends a 40 degree viewing angle for cinema - for 55-inch TV that is 1.7m distance. SMPTE recommends 30 degrees minimum - for 55-inch that is 2.3m. UK living room average viewing distance is 2.5-3m, suitable for 55-65 inch TVs. Use online TV height calculators (available free at rtings.com or whathifi.com) to enter your specific values.

---

## Room-Specific Mounting Heights for UK Homes

Living room (primary TV): centre at 105-115cm, distance 2.5-3m for 55-65 inch TV. Above fireplace: centre at 130-170cm with 15 degree tilting mount essential. Avoid this position for bedrooms as the high angle causes neck strain. Bedroom: centre at 120-140cm from floor, accounting for lying-down eye level. Use a full motion mount to angle the TV downward when lying in bed. Viewing distance in bedrooms is typically 2-4m from the bed. Kitchen: centre at 130-150cm for standing viewing angle. Small screen (32-43 inch) at 2-3m distance. Use a tilting mount for downward angle. Home office: centre at 105-115cm matching desk seated eye level. Screen at arm's length (60-80cm) for a 24-32 inch monitor. Gaming room: centre at 105-115cm for seated play. Distance 1.5-2.5m for 55-65 inch for immersive gaming. For multi-purpose rooms use a full motion mount to adjust screen position for different activities.
