# TV Mounting for 55 Inch TVs

55 inch TVs typically weigh 16-20kg and use 300x300mm or 400x400mm VESA patterns. This guide covers mount selection, wall type considerations, and installation specifics for this most popular UK TV size.

---

## Mount Selection for 55 Inch TVs

55 inch is the most popular TV size in UK homes, accounting for approximately 35% of TV sales. These TVs weigh 16-20kg depending on technology. LED LCD TVs weigh 16-18kg, OLED 55-inch weigh 15-17kg. Most 55-inch TVs use 300x300mm VESA pattern except Samsung QLED models which use 400x400mm and Sony Bravia which use 300x300mm. Popular UK models include LG OLED55C4 (16.3kg, 300x300mm), Samsung QE55QN90D (18.2kg, 400x400mm), Sony XR-55A95L (17.5kg, 300x300mm), and Hisense 55U8N (17.0kg, 300x300mm). For 55-inch TVs a fixed mount suits standard viewing. Use tilting mount for above eye level. Full motion for corner positions. Choose a mount rated at least 35kg for safety margin. ## Installation Guide for 55 Inch TVs

55-inch TVs require a helper due to size and weight. Step 1: Determine TV centre height at 105-115cm from floor for standard viewing. Step 2: Identify wall type using stud finder. UK brick walls need 10mm masonry drill bit and Fischer UX wall plugs. Stud walls need M8 coach screws into timber studs. Step 3: Mark bracket position using a laser level. Step 4: Drill pilot holes to correct depth (50-60mm for brick, 40mm for studs). Step 5: Insert wall fixings. Step 6: Attach VESA brackets to TV using M6x12mm screws supplied with mount. Step 7: Lift TV onto wall plate -- use TV lift assist handles (Â£12 from Amazon UK) if available. Step 8: Engage locking mechanism and verify stability. 55-inch TVs need minimum 20mm ventilation clearance from the wall. Cable management: use cable clips or brush outlet plates. Install a recessed single socket behind the mount for a clean install (Â£15 from Screwfix by a Part P electrician).

<dt>Can one person install a 55 inch TV mount?</dt>
<dd>A helper is strongly recommended. 55-inch TVs are large and awkward to lift onto a wall bracket alone. Use TV lift assist handles available from Amazon UK for Â£12 if installing solo.</dd>

<dt>What is the best mount type for a 55 inch TV?</dt>
<dd>Fixed mounts suit standard viewing (TV centre at 105-115cm). Use tilting mount for above-fireplace. Full motion mount suits corner positions or rooms where the viewing angle varies.</dd>
