# TV Mounting for 75-85 Inch TVs

75 to 85 inch TVs are the largest consumer TVs available weighing 30-45kg. This guide covers heavy-duty mount selection, wall structural assessment, multi-person installation, and professional installation recommendations for UK homes.

---

## Heavy Duty Mount Requirements for Large TVs

75-inch TVs weigh 30-35kg, 85-inch TVs weigh 35-45kg. VESA patterns are larger: 600x400mm is standard. Some models use 400x400mm with adapter plates. The wall plate must be substantial -- typically 600-800mm wide with 6-8 fixing points. Mount weight rating must be minimum 2x TV weight. For 85-inch at 45kg choose a mount rated 80-100kg.
