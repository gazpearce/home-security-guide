# Ultra-Slim TV Mounts for OLED TVs

Ultra-slim TV mounts hold the television just 10-15mm from the wall, creating a picture-frame appearance ideal for thin OLED and QD-OLED TVs. This guide covers compatibility, installation considerations, and UK-specific recommendations.

---

## OLED TV Mounting Requirements

OLED and QD-OLED TVs are significantly thinner than LED-LCD TVs — typically 4-8mm at the thinnest point vs 60-80mm for LED TVs. Their light weight (55-inch OLED ~16kg vs LED ~18kg) allows ultra-slim mounts with lower weight ratings. However OLED screens are more fragile and require careful handling. The VESA pattern on OLED TVs varies: LG OLEDs typically use 300x300mm for 55-65 inch while Sony and Panasonic use 400x400mm. Always verify VESA compatibility before purchasing. Ultra-slim mounts for OLEDs have profile depths of 10-15mm using low-profile brackets that attach directly to the VESA points. The trade-off is zero rear access — connect all cables before mounting. Popular UK ultra-slim mounts: Vogel\'s Thin 545 (TÜV certified, up to 35kg, 11mm profile), B-Tech BT5511 (up to 30kg, 14mm profile), and Sanus ELMF2 (up to 45kg, 15mm profile).

---

## Installation Considerations for Ultra-Slim Mounts

Installation follows standard wall mounting procedure but with critical differences. Because the TV sits so close to the wall, standard HDMI cables may not fit — use right-angle or ultra-slim HDMI adapters. Power cables must be routed carefully; some mounts include a recessed channel for the power cable. Ensure wall surface is perfectly flat — any unevenness can stress the thin OLED panel. For plasterboard walls, use cavity fixings that provide a flush surface. The TV\'s ventilation must be considered: OLEDs generate heat from the rear and need some airflow. While a 10-15mm gap is usually sufficient, check the TV manual for minimum clearance. Some LG OLED TVs specifically recommend at least 20mm clearance. Ultra-slim mounts cost £25-£60 from UK retailers like John Lewis, Richer Sounds, and Amazon UK.

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Will an ultra-slim mount work with my LG OLED?</dt>
<dd>Most LG OLEDs use VESA 300x300mm for 55-65 inch models and 400x400mm for 77 inch. Ultra-slim mounts support these patterns. Check your specific model in the LG user manual. Some LG OLEDs have recessed VESA points requiring extended spacers.</dd>

<dt>Do I need special HDMI cables for ultra-slim mounting?</dt>
<dd>Yes — standard HDMI cables with thick moulded ends often do not fit in the tight 10-15mm gap. Use right-angle HDMI adapters (£5-£10) or ultra-slim flat HDMI cables. Connect all cables before mounting the TV.</dd>

<dt>Is 10-15mm enough ventilation for OLED TVs?</dt>
<dd>Most OLED TVs require minimum 10mm rear clearance for ventilation. Some LG models specifically recommend 20mm. Check your TV manual. If minimum clearance exceeds the mount depth, choose a slightly thicker mount.</dd>

<dt>Can I use an ultra-slim mount with a soundbar?</dt>
<dd>Ultra-slim mounts leave no room for a soundbar behind the TV. Mount the soundbar separately on the wall below the TV or use a soundbar bracket that attaches to the TV\'s bottom edge.</dd>

<dt>Are ultra-slim mounts compatible with QD-OLED TVs?</dt>
<dd>Yes — QD-OLED TVs from Sony and Samsung use the same VESA patterns as standard OLEDs. The weight is similar. Ensure the mount supports the specific VESA pattern and TV weight. Samsung QD-OLEDs use 300x300mm for 55-65 inch. For more information see our <a href="01-fixed-tv-mount-installation-guide.md">Fixed TV Mount Installation Guide</a>.</dd>
</dl>
