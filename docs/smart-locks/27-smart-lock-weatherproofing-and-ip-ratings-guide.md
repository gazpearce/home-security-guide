## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>What IP rating do I need for a UK front door?</dt>
<dd>IP44 minimum for most UK locations. IP54 or higher for exposed doors facing prevailing wind or rain. IP65 for coastal properties.</dd>

<dt>Does cold weather affect smart lock batteries?</dt>
<dd>Yes - alkaline batteries lose 30-50% capacity below -5C. Use lithium AA batteries for reliable winter operation. Lock motor also works harder in winter.</dd>

<dt>How do I prevent condensation on my smart lock?</dt>
<dd>Ensure the weather cover rubber gasket is fitted correctly. Apply silicone sealant around the keypad edge. Install under a porch canopy if possible. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/26-smart-lock-installation-without-screws-guide.md">Smart Lock Installation Without Screws Guide</a>.</dd>

</dl>
