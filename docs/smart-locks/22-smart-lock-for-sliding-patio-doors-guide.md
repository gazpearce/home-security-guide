## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Can any sliding door be made smart?</dt>
<dd>Most sliding patio doors with a lock mechanism can be retrofitted with a smart solution. Check your cylinder type first. Non-standard mechanisms may need a surface-mounted actuator.</dd>

<dt>Is the installation the same as standard smart locks?</dt>
<dd>Not always - sliding door mechanisms differ from standard euro cylinder doors. Surface-mounted actuators and handle replacements require different installation steps.</dd>

<dt>Will my sliding door smart lock work in winter?</dt>
<dd>Yes, but UK winter conditions stiffen rubber seals. Ensure IP44+ rating. Lubricate the locking mechanism with graphite quarterly. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/21-smart-lock-for-french-doors-uk.md">Smart Lock for French Doors UK</a>.</dd>

</dl>
