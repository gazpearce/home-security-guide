## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Can I put a smart lock on only one French door leaf?</dt>
<dd>Yes - the active leaf gets the smart lock. The passive leaf must be manually shootbolted. Both leaves must be secured for full security.</dd>

<dt>Do I need two smart locks for French doors?</dt>
<dd>Not necessarily - one smart lock on the active leaf with a manual shootbolt on the passive leaf works. Dual locks provide full remote control of both leaves.</dd>

<dt>How do UK seasons affect French door smart locks?</dt>
<dd>Wooden French doors swell in summer and contract in winter. Check alignment quarterly and adjust hinges to prevent stiff operation or auto-lock failure. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/20-smart-lock-maintenance-and-troubleshooting-uk.md">Smart Lock Maintenance and Troubleshooting UK</a>.</dd>

</dl>
