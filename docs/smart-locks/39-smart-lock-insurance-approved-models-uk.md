## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Does my home insurance require a specific smart lock standard?</dt>
<dd>Most UK insurers require TS 007 3-star or Sold Secure Gold minimum. Check your policy wording. Ultion Diamond and Avocet Gold exceed the minimum requirement.</dd>

<dt>Can I get an insurance discount for installing a smart lock?</dt>
<dd>Yes - typically 5-15% discount. Contact your insurer to declare the upgrade. Provide the TS 007 or Sold Secure certification details.</dd>

<dt>What if my smart lock is not TS 007 3-star?</dt>
<dd>You can upgrade the cylinder separately. Buy a TS 007 3-star euro cylinder (£30-£60) that is compatible with your smart lock. This satisfies insurance requirements. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/38-smart-lock-installation-for-metal-doors-guide.md">Smart Lock Installation for Metal Doors Guide</a>.</dd>

</dl>
