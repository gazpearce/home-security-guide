## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Can I use key fobs without a smartphone?</dt>
<dd>Yes - fobs are programmed via the app but used independently. Once programmed, the fob works without the phone. Users tap the fob on the lock reader.</dd>

<dt>What happens if I lose my key fob?</dt>
<dd>Immediately deactivate it in the app. The fob is instantly rendered useless. Buy a replacement fob and reprogram it. Keep backup fobs in a secure location.</dd>

<dt>Are key fobs more secure than PIN codes?</dt>
<dd>Comparable - fobs use encrypted communication. The main risk is loss of the physical fob. Fobs with activity logging provide an audit trail of every use. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/39-smart-lock-insurance-approved-models-uk.md">Smart Lock Insurance Approved Models UK</a>.</dd>

</dl>
