## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>How long is the smart lock warranty?</dt>
<dd>Typically 2 years. Ultion offers the best UK warranty at 5 years. Register your product within 30 days for full coverage.</dd>

<dt>What is covered under UK consumer law?</dt>
<dd>Consumer Rights Act 2015 gives you up to 6 years (England/Wales) to claim for faults present at purchase. The retailer is responsible for first 30 days full refund.</dd>

<dt>What is NOT covered by smart lock warranty?</dt>
<dd>Batteries, weather damage beyond IP rating, incorrect installation damage, normal wear and tear after 2-3 years, and cosmetic damage. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/45-smart-lock-annual-maintenance-checklist.md">Smart Lock Annual Maintenance Checklist</a>.</dd>
