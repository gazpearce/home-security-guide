## Specifications and Comparison

| No-Drill Method | Best For | Bond Strength | Cure Time | Removal Ease |
| ---|---|---|---|--- |
| 3M VHB tape | uPVC, smooth timber, composite | 10kg per 4cm strip | 24-48 hours | Heat + pry, residue removable |
| Epoxy adhesive | Textured timber, GRP | Very strong | 24 hours | Difficult, may leave residue |
| Clamp-on system | Door edge mounting | Mechanical grip | Instant | Easy, fully removable |
| Magnetic escutcheon | Metal doors | Moderate | Instant | Very easy, no residue |
| Adhesive + silicone sealant | Outdoor, weather-exposed | Enhanced weather seal | 48 hours | Requires cutting sealant |

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Will adhesive damage my door when removed?</dt>
<dd>VHB tape can be removed with heat and leaves minimal residue that cleans off with isopropyl alcohol. Epoxy may leave marks on painted doors.</dd>

<dt>How strong is adhesive mounting for smart locks?</dt>
<dd>3M VHB tape holds 10kg per 4cm strip - sufficient for all consumer smart locks. Follow the 24-hour cure time before bearing load.</dd>

<dt>Can I install a smart lock without tools?</dt>
<dd>Yes - adhesive-mounted locks require no tools. The provided tape or adhesive plate attaches to the door surface. No drilling, screws, or door modification needed. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/25-smart-lock-for-rental-properties-uk.md">Smart Lock for Rental Properties UK</a>.</dd>

</dl>
