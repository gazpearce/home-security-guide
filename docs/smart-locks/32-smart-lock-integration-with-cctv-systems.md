## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Can I record video whenever my smart lock is used?</dt>
<dd>Yes - integrate your lock with CCTV. When the lock unlocks, the nearest camera starts recording. This creates a video record of everyone entering.</dd>

<dt>What is the best platform for lock-camera integration?</dt>
<dd>Home Assistant provides the most flexible cross-brand integration. Ring and Hive offer easy native integration but only within their ecosystems.</dd>

<dt>Is CCTV integration with smart locks legally compliant?</dt>
<dd>Yes if CCTV is registered with ICO where required. The lock activity log and camera footage combination provides a comprehensive court-admissible audit trail. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/31-smart-lock-delivery-driver-access-guide.md">Smart Lock Delivery Driver Access Guide</a>.</dd>

</dl>
