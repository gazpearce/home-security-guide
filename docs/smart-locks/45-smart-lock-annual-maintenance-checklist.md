## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>How often should I replace smart lock batteries?</dt>
<dd>Replace proactively every 12 months regardless of app reading. Check monthly via app - replace when below 30%. Use lithium AAs for longest life.</dd>

<dt>Can I service my smart lock myself?</dt>
<dd>Yes - all maintenance tasks are DIY. For cylinder removal and hinge adjustment, basic tools are needed. Contact a locksmith only if the mechanism is seized or damaged.</dd>

<dt>How long should a smart lock last?</dt>
<dd>Expected lifespan 5-10 years. The euro cylinder may need replacing at 5 years (mechanical wear). The electronics typically last 7-10 years. For more information see our <a href="../smart-locks/44-smart-lock-multi-property-management-guide.md">Smart Lock Multi-Property Management Guide</a>.</dd>

</dl>
