# CCTV Systems

CCTV (Closed-Circuit Television) systems are a cornerstone of modern home security.

## Types of Cameras

- **Dome Cameras** — Discreet, vandal-resistant, ideal for indoor use
- **Bullet Cameras** — Visible deterrent, long-range, weatherproof
- **PTZ Cameras** — Pan-tilt-zoom, remote-controlled viewing
- **Wireless Cameras** — Easy installation, Wi-Fi connected

## Legal Requirements (UK)

- ICO registration may be required
- GDPR compliance for data storage
- Signage must be displayed
- Privacy considerations for neighbouring properties

## Installation Tips

Position cameras at entry points, cover all blind spots, and ensure adequate lighting for night vision.
