# Outdoor Security for UK Homes

Outdoor security lighting, alarms, and sensors form the first line of defence for any property. ONS crime statistics show 42% of burglaries involve rear entry, making garden and perimeter security essential. PIR security lights, LED floodlights, driveway alarms, and trail cameras provide cost-effective protection. Solar-powered options are increasingly viable with larger battery capacities and efficient LED arrays.

## Key Considerations for UK Properties

- **Lighting Placement**: Mount PIR lights at 2.5-3 metres; LED floodlights at 3-4 metres angled 30° downward
- **IP Ratings**: Minimum IP65 for wall-mounted lights and cameras; IP66/IP67 for ground-level or exposed locations
- **Driveway Alarms**: PIR sensors (£15-£40), infrared beams (£50-£150), or buried detectors (£100-£300)
- **Gate Security**: 5-lever mortice deadlocks (BS3621), closed-shackle padlocks, and anti-lift hinge bolts
- **Trail Cameras**: Covert monitoring at £40-£200 — legal on your property if not aimed at neighbours or public spaces
