# Home Networking Guide UK

A well-designed home network is essential for modern UK homes with 13+ connected devices. Whether you have FTTC (30-80 Mbps) or FTTP (500-1000 Mbps), the right combination of router, mesh system, and structured cabling ensures fast, reliable connectivity throughout your property. Ofcom data shows wired Ethernet cuts latency by up to 80% compared to WiFi for gaming and 4K streaming.

## Key Considerations for UK Properties

- **Broadband Type**: FTTP is strongly preferred — symmetrical speeds up to 900 Mbps, available to 65%+ of UK premises
- **Mesh WiFi**: Tri-band WiFi 6/6E systems like TP-Link Deco and Netgear Orbi eliminate dead zones in Victorian layouts
- **Structured Cabling**: Cat6a supports 10 Gbps up to 100 metres — ideal for media rooms, office, and gaming setups
- **Powerline**: AV1000 adapters deliver 200-400 Mbps real-world — best for homes where running Ethernet is impractical
- **Security**: WPA3, guest network isolation, and regular firmware updates are essential for UK smart homes
