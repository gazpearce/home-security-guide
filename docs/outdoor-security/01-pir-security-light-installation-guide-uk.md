# PIR Security Light Installation Guide UK

Step-by-step guide to installing PIR security lights around your UK home to deter intruders and illuminate dark areas effectively.

---

## Choosing the Right PIR Security Light

PIR lights from Screwfix, B&Q, and Toolstation cost £15 - £80 for standard LED units; the Ring Floodlight Wired Pro (£179) adds 1080p camera integration. Most UK homes install two units covering front and rear approaches. ONS data shows 42% of burglaries involve rear entry, making back-garden lighting essential for perimeter defence.

---

## Installation Steps for UK Homes

Isolate mains at the consumer unit before starting. Run 1.5mm SWA cable from an indoor RCD spur and drill a 20mm hole through the external wall. Mount the light at 2.5 - 3 metres on a solid wall; adjust timer to 1 - 5 minutes and sensitivity to medium. Connect three-core cable (brown, blue, green/yellow) and test after resetting the RCD at dusk.

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>What height should a PIR security light be mounted?</dt>
<dd>Mount between 2.5 and 3 metres high for optimal ground-level detection without triggering on small animals.</dd>

<dt>Do PIR lights deter burglars?</dt>
<dd>Yes  -  ONS statistics confirm 62% of burglars avoid properties with visible security lighting, making PIR lights a cost-effective deterrent.</dd>

<dt>Can I install a PIR light without an electrician?</dt>
<dd>Not if wiring into the mains  -  Part P of UK Building Regulations requires certification for new circuits. Plug-in or battery units can be DIY-installed. For more information see our <a href="02-led-floodlight-installation-guide-uk.md">LED Floodlight Installation Guide UK</a>.</dd>
</dl>
