# Driveway Alarm Installation Guide UK

Guide to driveway alarm systems for UK homes including wireless sensors, driveway beams, and vehicle detection for gates and private roads.

---

## Driveway Alarm Types for UK Properties

Driveway alarms range from basic PIR sensors (£15 - £40 from Screwfix) to infrared beam kits (£50 - £150) and buried vehicle detectors (£100 - £300). Brands include Yale, Optex, and Dakota Alert. A typical UK driveway is 3 - 6 metres wide and 5 - 15 metres long  -  sufficient for most wireless systems with ranges up to 200 metres. ONS data confirms 22% of burglaries involve vehicles.

---

## Installation Tips for UK Driveways

For PIR sensors, mount 2 metres high on a wall or post at the driveway entrance. Infrared beams require direct line-of-sight across the drive. Buried vehicle detectors need a 50mm hole in tarmac or concrete. For gravel drives, install a pressure-mat sensor under 5 - 10cm of gravel. Pair wireless receivers with a wired 12V DC adaptor for reliability. Test range before final mounting  -  solid stone walls reduce signal strength significantly.

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Do driveway alarms work with electric gates?</dt>
<dd>Yes  -  most wireless alarms integrate with gate controllers. Buried detectors are ideal for automatic gate activation.</dd>

<dt>Will my neighbour's car trigger my driveway alarm?</dt>
<dd>Proper sensor aim and narrow detection zones reduce false triggers. Adjustable beam widths help isolate your driveway.</dd>

<dt>Are driveway alarms weatherproof?</dt>
<dd>UK-rated outdoor sensors carry IP44 - IP66 certification. Ensure wireless receivers are indoors or in a weatherproof housing. For more information see our <a href="01-pir-security-light-installation-guide-uk.md">PIR Security Light Installation Guide UK</a>.</dd>
</dl>
