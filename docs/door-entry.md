# Video Doorbell and Door Entry Systems UK

Video doorbells and intercom systems let you see, hear, and speak to visitors from anywhere via your smartphone. For UK homes, key considerations include transformer voltage for wired models, weatherproofing for British climate, WiFi signal strength through brick walls, and ICO privacy compliance when recording beyond your property boundary. The UK market is dominated by Ring, Nest, Eufy, and Hikvision systems.

## Key Considerations for UK Properties

- **Transformer Requirements**: Video doorbells need 16-24V AC at 20VA minimum — most UK doorbell transformers are underspecified
- **WiFi at the Front Door**: 2.4 GHz is the only reliable choice through solid brick walls; measure RSSI before buying
- **Mounting Height**: 100-120 cm for optimal face and package capture — ICO guidance requires avoiding public pavement
- **Battery Life**: Expect 3-4 months in summer, 6-8 weeks in winter — solar panels offset up to 40% of drain
- **Chime Bypass**: UK mechanical chimes require a 10Ω 10W resistor to prevent humming
