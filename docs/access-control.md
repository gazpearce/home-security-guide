# Access Control Systems for UK Homes

Access control systems manage entry to your property — from driveway gates and pedestrian gates to car park barriers and communal entrances. For UK homeowners, the right system balances security, convenience, and compliance with British Standards and UK building regulations. This guide covers gate automation, keypads, intercoms, RFID readers, biometrics, and planning permission requirements.

## Key Considerations for UK Properties

- **Gate Types**: Swing gates suit flat driveways with depth; sliding gates work on sloping or short driveways
- **Compliance**: BS EN 12453 mandates two independent safety devices on all automated gates since 2025
- **Electrical Work**: Mains-powered installations require Part P notification or a registered competent person
- **Planning Permission**: Gates under 2m set back 1m+ from the highway are typically permitted development
- **Battery Backup**: 24V DC systems with battery backup keep gates operational during power cuts
