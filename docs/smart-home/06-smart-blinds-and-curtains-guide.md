# Smart Blinds and Curtains Guide

Guide to smart blinds and curtains for UK homes covering battery-powered options, solar charging, IKEA FYRTUR, Eve MotionBlinds, integration with dawn simulation, and energy-saving automation.

---

## Smart Blind Types and UK Compatibility

Three types: battery-powered (most common, rechargeable), solar-powered (south-facing UK windows), mains-powered (professional install). Popular: IKEA FYRTUR (£100-£150 per blind, Zigbee, from IKEA) - most affordable for standard UK sizes. Eve MotionBlinds (£150-£250, Thread, Apple HomeKit). Lutron Serena (£300-£600, professional install) - premium. Smart roller blinds are most common UK configuration. Vertical smart blinds and tracks available for patio doors and bay windows from Lutron and Somfy.

---

## Installation and Automation Setup

Step 1: Measure window width and drop in mm. IKEA FYRTUR fits pre-set UK sizes. Eve custom-ordered. Step 2: Choose power - battery for most, solar for south-facing, mains for new builds. Step 3: Mount bracket to window frame or reveal. Step 4: Clip blind onto bracket. Step 5: Charge battery and install. Step 6: Pair with app/hub. Step 7: Automate - sunrise/sunset schedules, scenes (Good Morning), geofencing. Energy-saving: close south blinds in summer to reduce solar heat gain by 30%. Open in winter for passive heating. Estimated savings: £50-£150/year.

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Can smart blinds fit UK uPVC windows?</dt>
<dd>Yes - most mount to the window reveal with brackets or adhesive. IKEA FYRTUR fits standard UK sizes.</dd>

<dt>How long do batteries last?</dt>
<dd>3-6 months depending on usage. Typical UK usage (open/close once daily) gives 4-5 months.</dd>

<dt>Do smart blinds save energy?</dt>
<dd>Yes - automating to close south blinds in summer and open in winter saves £50-£150/year. For more information see our <a href="../smart-home/05-smart-sensors-door-window-motion-guide-uk.md">Smart Sensors Door Window Motion Guide UK</a>.</dd>
