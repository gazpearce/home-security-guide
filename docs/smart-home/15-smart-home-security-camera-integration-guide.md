# Smart Home Security Camera Integration Guide

Guide to integrating security cameras with UK smart home platforms covering Ring, Eufy, Hive, Arlo camera setup, routines, video doorbell integration, and local vs cloud storage options.

---

## Camera Integration with Smart Home Platforms

Security cameras enhance smart home security when integrated with your platform. Ring cameras integrate with Alexa - view live feed on Echo Show, receive motion alerts, trigger routines (lights on when camera detects motion). Integration requires Ring skill in Alexa. Eufy cameras work with both Alexa and Google - view via smart displays, receive notifications. No subscription needed for local storage. Hive cameras integrate within Hive ecosystem - camera, alarm, heating, lights all in one app. Arlo cameras integrate with Alexa, Google, and Apple HomeKit - rich notifications, smart display viewing, routine triggers. All platforms support: motion-triggered recording, push notifications, smart display live view, and routine integration. Camera feeds appear on Echo Show and Nest Hub when doorbell is pressed.

---

## Camera Automation Routines and Storage

Security routines with cameras: Arrival: disarm camera motion detection, turn off notifications. Departure: arm cameras, enable motion notifications, send snapshot to phone. Night: enable floodlight cameras, increase sensitivity, send alerts. Package detected (Ring): announce package arrived via smart speaker, turn on porch light. Camera-triggered: when motion detected at front door after sunset, turn on hall light, show feed on Echo Show, send 10-second clip to phone. Local storage (Eufy, Hive): Keep footage on local hard drive or base station - no monthly fee. Cloud storage (Ring): subscription required (£3.50/month for Ring Protect) for recording and sharing. Hybrid: Arlo offers local storage via SmartHub plus optional cloud. For UK homes, local storage avoids ICO registration concerns - less personal data transmitted to cloud servers. Camera placement for UK homes: front door (face level for ID), driveway (overview, number plate angle), back garden (gate and patio coverage), side boundaries (narrow field).

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Can I view camera feed on my smart display?</dt>
<dd>Yes - Echo Show shows Ring and Eufy feeds. Nest Hub shows Nest and compatible cameras. Apple TV shows HomeKit Secure Video.</dd>

<dt>Do I need a subscription for security cameras?</dt>
<dd>Eufy stores locally with no subscription. Ring requires £3.50/month for recording. Arlo has optional subscription.</dd>

<dt>Can camera motion trigger smart home actions?</dt>
<dd>Yes - Alexa Routines can turn on lights, sound alarm, or send announcement when camera detects motion. For more information see our <a href="../smart-home/14-smart-home-multi-room-audio-guide.md">Smart Home Multi-Room Audio Guide</a>.</dd>
