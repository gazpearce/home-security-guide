# Smart Thermostat Installation Guide UK

Guide to installing and configuring smart thermostats for UK homes covering Nest, Hive, Tado, Drayton Wiser installations, TRV setup, geofencing, and energy savings from the Energy Saving Trust.

---

## Choosing a Smart Thermostat for UK Heating Systems

Smart thermostats save 10-30% on heating bills per the Energy Saving Trust. Four major UK options: Google Nest Learning (£200-£250, auto-schedule), Hive Active (£180-£220, Hive ecosystem), Tado Smart (£200-£280, excellent geofencing + TRVs), Drayton Wiser (£150-£200, budget British option). All work with combi boilers, system boilers, and heat pumps. Nest works with most UK combi boilers (Vaillant, Worcester Bosch, Ideal, Baxi). Hive is UK-designed. Tado works with all major UK brands.

---

## Installation and Configuration

Step 1: Switch off mains power. Step 2: Remove existing thermostat - photograph wiring first. Step 3: Install the receiver/Heat Link. For Nest: Heat Link connects to boiler. For Hive: receiver replaces timer. For Tado: wireless receiver connects to boiler. Step 4: Wire new thermostat - apps provide wiring guides. Step 5: Mount centrally, away from sun/radiators/draughts. Step 6: Restore power and follow app setup. Step 7: Configure schedule: 21C evening, 18C away, 16C night. Step 8: Enable geofencing. Step 9: Add smart TRVs (£40-£60 each) for room control. Step 10: Set frost protection below 7C.

## FAQ

<dl>

<dt>Can I install a smart thermostat myself?</dt>
<dd>It involves mains voltage wiring. DIY if confident, otherwise hire a qualified electrician or Gas Safe engineer.</dd>

<dt>Will it work with my combi boiler?</dt>
<dd>Most UK combi boilers are compatible. Check the manufacturers online compatibility checker.</dd>

<dt>How much can I save?</dt>
<dd>Energy Saving Trust estimates 10-30% on heating bills. For a typical UK 3-bed home spending £1,000-£1,500/year, savings of £100-£450. For more information see our <a href="../smart-home/02-smart-lighting-installation-guide.md">Smart Lighting Installation Guide</a>.</dd>
